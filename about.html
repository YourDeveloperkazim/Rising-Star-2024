About Us

A professional cricket academy imparting coaching in the game of cricket. Rising Star Cricket Academy envisions the grooming of cricket aspirants to become professionals in the game using a mix of both conventional and contemporary coaching methods rendered by professionally accredited coaches in an environment that is designed to bring out the best in the players.

Vision

  • To provide correct training to the underprivileged children and groom them to participate for prestigious tournament like The Ranji Trophy, The Indian Premier League and also help them to achieve the dream to represent in the Indian National Cricket team.
  • Also provide correct training to the girls and give them equal opportunity to achieve their dream to play in various tournaments as professional women cricket team for our country.

Core Team

The core team of Rising Star Cricket Academy consists of experienced and dedicated professionals who are passionate about nurturing young cricket talent.

FARHAD DARUWALA
FOUNDER
Worked with Rajasthan Royals, Cricket Canada and Ranji Teams

Farhad Daruwala is associated with the World Cricket Academy (WCA) for the past 16 years offering cricket coaching and has now graduated into the position as Assistant Director of Coaching.

ZUBIN BHARUCHA
MENTOR
Director/Strategy, Development & Performance Rajasthan Royals

An Indian former first-class cricketer who played for Bombay and Surrey County Cricket Club. He worked as the team director of Rajasthan Royals from 2008 to 2015, and is working as Director of Cricket since 2018.

ROHINTON IRANPUR
ADVISOR
Rising Star Cricket Academy

Since 22nd September 2013, Mr. Rohinton Iranpur comes all the way from California to participate in the Dr Kanga League Tournament Mr. Rohinton Iranpur is coming diligently every year since 2013 to boost the Parsi Cyclist team.

NEVILLE WADIA
TRAINER
Specializes in performance enhancement, exercise prescription, rehab and prehab

Neville is the founder of Soleus, a health and fitness studio in Mumbai setup in early January 2018. He is a qualified Exercise Professional from Fitness Australia and a postgraduate MBA from the University of South Australia. Neville specialises in performance enhancement, exercise prescription, rehab, prehab, and working with special populations. He is also a qualified Master Rehab Trainer from the Rehab Trainer Institute in Australia. He has pioneered Hypoxic (Simulated Altitude Training) in India, setting up facilities with Low Oxygen rooms to train high performance athletes, and those with medical conditions like diabetes and obesity. He also worked in Australia for a year with the Australian Olympic Swimmers (Queensland) pre-London Olympics.

ADIL MITHAIWALA
CONSULTANT
Former Indian Cricketer

Adil Mithaiwala is a former Indian cricketer who played for the Indian national cricket team. He was a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-fast bowler. He played for the Indian national cricket team from 1996 to 2000. He was a part of the Indian team that won the 1996 Cricket World Cup.
